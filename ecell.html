ABOUT US

E-Summit is the flagship event of Entrepreneurship Cell at NITK. Envision to create an entrepreneurial sprit amongst the next generation of graduates ;it is a multi-faceted event that encompasses Entrepreneurship talks ,business workshops,panel discussions and a pitching event .

We invite changemakers from various spheres of work including journalism , entertainment,social Entrepreneurship, politics as well as well established business to serve as mentors for these events

E-TALKS

The very idea of E-Summit is to inspire - to call out to the Entrepreneur in you and challenge you to take the big entrepreneurial leap. Through E-talks we provide a platform for Entrepreneurs to take us on their journey along the long treacherous road to ultimate self actualisation ,showing us that Entrepreneurshipnot only empowers the Entrepreneurbut also society at large.
